# Print QA Checklist (Calendar)

Use this checklist after opening **Print Preview** for a month calendar.

- Confirm orientation is **Landscape**.
- Confirm the **month title** appears at the top and matches the selected month/year.
- Confirm the calendar shows a **7-column grid** (Sunday through Saturday).
- Confirm each day has a visible **day box** (no missing or collapsed cells).
- Confirm scheduled **assignment text** appears inside the correct day boxes and is readable.
- Confirm **Admin controls** and **text preview/output panels** are hidden in print view.
